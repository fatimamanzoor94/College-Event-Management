Cultural Event

Music Fest

November 15-17, 2025
4:00 PM - 11:00 PM
College Amphitheater

Get ready for the most electrifying musical experience of the year! Our annual Music Fest brings together talented artists, bands, and performers for three days of non-stop entertainment and celebration.

About the Music Fest

The Music Fest is a three-day celebration of music, art, and culture. This event showcases a diverse range of musical genres and performances, from rock and pop to classical and fusion. Whether you're a music enthusiast or just looking for a great time, our festival offers something for everyone. Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with live performances, food stalls, and vibrant atmosphere.

Featured Performances

  • Rock Bands Showdown
  • Acoustic Sessions
  • DJ Nights & Electronic Music
  • Classical Music Ensemble
  • Folk & Traditional Performances
  • Battle of the Bands Competition
  • Solo Artist Showcase
  • Fusion Music Collaborations

Who Should Attend

This festival is open to all students, faculty, staff, and music lovers from the community. Whether you're a performer or just want to enjoy the music, you're welcome to join us. Food and beverage stalls will be available throughout the event. Don't forget to bring your friends and family for this incredible musical journey!
