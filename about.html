Event Timeline

A monthly overview of our major events to help you plan your participation throughout the academic year.

Aug

Freshers' Welcome

Orientation program for new students with cultural performances and introductions to college clubs. It serves to introduce new students to the college's facilities, faculty, and academic systems, fostering a sense of belonging and community. The event typically includes welcome speeches, entertainment, social activities, and sometimes guidance from seniors to help freshers feel comfortable, confident, and connected to their new academic journey

Sep

TechFest

Annual technology festival featuring coding competitions, project exhibitions, and tech workshops. A college tech fest is an annual student-organized event focused on promoting innovation, showcasing technical skills, and fostering interest in science and technology through competitions, exhibitions, and workshops, often including fun elements like gaming and simulators to engage participants.

Nov

Cultural Night

An evening of music, dance, and drama performances showcasing student talents. A college culture night is an event celebrating the diversity of a college community by showcasing the unique traditions, arts, and heritage of its students. Organized primarily by students and international programs, it features a variety of performances, exhibitions, and food, fostering an inclusive atmosphere and promoting cross-cultural understanding.

Jan

Sports Meet

Inter-college athletic competition with track and field events, team sports, and closing ceremony. Events like sprints (100m, 200m), long jump, shot put, and relay races. Competitions such as football, cricket, badminton (singles and doubles), and netball. Activities like tug-of-war, a three-legged race, and a spoon race, fostering camaraderie and inclusivity.

Mar

Annual Day

Celebration of academic achievements with award ceremonies, guest speakers, and cultural events. A college annual day is a festive annual event celebrating a college's achievements, where students, faculty, and guests gather for performances like dance, music, and drama, along with award ceremonies for academic and extracurricular excellence.
