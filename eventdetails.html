Healthcare Event

Blood Donation Drive

November 15, 2025
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
College Health Center

Join us for our annual Blood Donation Drive and help save lives! This is your opportunity to make a real difference in our community by donating the gift of life.

About the Blood Donation Drive

The Blood Donation Drive is a humanitarian initiative aimed at collecting blood donations to support local hospitals and blood banks. This event promotes awareness about the importance of blood donation and provides a safe, convenient way for eligible donors to contribute to this life-saving cause. Every donation can help save up to three lives!

Donation Process

  • Registration - Fill out a donor registration form
  • Health Screening - Brief health check and questionnaire
  • Donation - The actual blood donation process (about 10 minutes)
  • Refreshments - Enjoy snacks and beverages after donation
  • Rest - A short rest period before leaving

Who Can Donate

Donors must be between 18-60 years of age, weigh at least 50 kg, and be in good health. Potential donors will be screened for eligibility on the day of the event. Please bring a valid ID proof and eat a good meal before donating. For specific eligibility questions, please consult with our medical staff at the event.

Donation Drive Information

No Registration Fee
Certificate of Appreciation for All Donors
Free Health Check-up
Refreshments and Post-Donation Care
Professional Medical Staff On-site
